Gaining Access to GPIRS

Native GPIRS

To use Native GPIRS, which includes all of GPIRS functionality, you must be registered for access and install the GPIRS software on your computer.  

(You can gain access to GPIRS for Yourself and/or your supervisor(s) by:

(1 Download the 'Registration Form'

(2 Send the signed, completed form to GPIRS Administrator. Click here for a list of Local Administrators.

GPIRS on the web ('My Parts Procurement')

GPIRS on the web ('My Parts Procurement') is designed to serve the Engineering community and its functionality is limited to:

  • Creating and managing RPS orders

  • Managing material requests

  • Managing shipping requests

NOTE:   To use GPIRS on the web ('My Parts Procurement') your supervisor must be  registered on GPIRS
